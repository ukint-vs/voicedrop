Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

VoiceDrop records audio and transcribes it on your device using Apple's Neural Engine via the open-source FluidAudio library (Parakeet TDT v3 model from NVIDIA, MIT license). Audio data and transcripts are never transmitted to us or to any third party.

What we store locally

  • Transcripts you create, saved to your device's Documents directory. Delete them anytime from the History tab or by deleting the app.
  • The speech model file, cached after a one-time download from Hugging Face's public model hub on first use (~600 MB).
  • Your app settings (silence detection, auto-copy, language mode), stored in UserDefaults on your device.

Shared App Group storage

VoiceDrop's keyboard extension and main app share a small UserDefaults store (identifier group.com.ukintvs.voicedrop) to hand off transcribed text between processes. Entries have a short time-to-live:

  • Keyboard insertion text: 2 minutes, single-consume (cleared the moment the keyboard reads it to insert at your cursor).
  • Deferred pasteboard text: 10 minutes, cleared after the main app writes it to the system pasteboard.

Both stores are cleared when you uninstall the app.

Network activity

The only network request VoiceDrop makes is a one-time download of the speech model from huggingface.co during onboarding or first use. After the model is cached, VoiceDrop runs entirely offline.

The FluidAudio library also fetches a small Silero VAD model (silence detection) from the same source on first use. Same offline-after-cache behavior.

No tracking, no analytics, no third-party SDKs collecting data

VoiceDrop does not integrate any analytics SDK (Firebase, Segment, Amplitude, etc.) and does not send usage data to any server. Apple's TestFlight and App Store Connect aggregate crash and performance reports via MetricKit, delivered to the developer as part of the normal iOS app lifecycle; these reports are subject to Apple's privacy policy. You can opt out of sharing these reports in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements on your iPhone.

Data deletion

Delete the VoiceDrop app to remove all locally stored data. Transcripts, the model cache, settings, and App Group storage are all cleared on uninstall.

Third-party components

  • FluidAudio (MIT) — open-source speech recognition, runs on-device.
  • OnboardingKit (MIT) — SwiftUI onboarding UI, no data collection.
  • Pow (MIT) — SwiftUI animation effects, no data collection.
  • Parakeet TDT v3 (CC-BY-4.0) — NVIDIA speech model, runs on-device.
  • Silero VAD (MIT) — silence detection model, runs on-device.
