VoiceDrop

Private voice-to-text for iPhone.

Record, transcribe, and insert text anywhere — without your voice ever leaving your device.

VoiceDrop transcribes speech locally on your iPhone using Apple's Neural Engine and the open-source Parakeet model. No cloud, no analytics, no account. The keyboard extension inserts the transcript at your cursor in any app that accepts text.

  • On-device only Audio and transcripts never leave your phone. Only the speech model itself is downloaded once from Hugging Face during onboarding.
  • 25 languages English, Russian, Spanish, German, French, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Hindi, and sixteen more — all offline after the first download.
  • Three ways to dictate Open the app and tap the mic, press the Action Button from anywhere, or use the VoiceDrop keyboard inside any messaging app.
  • No tracking, no SDKs Zero analytics integrations. No third-party data collection. TestFlight crash reports go through Apple's own MetricKit and can be opted out at the iOS level.
